About FIRST LEGO League (FLL)

FIRST LEGO League introduces STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) to children ages 4-16 through hands-on learning experiences. Our program offers real-world problem-solving opportunities, fostering critical thinking, teamwork, and innovation.

Season Theme: DIVE - The Ocean Awaits

In the 2024-2025 FIRST season, FIRST® DIVE℠ presented by Qualcomm, teams will explore life beneath the surface of the ocean. Our theme, SUBMERGED℠, challenges young minds to dive deep into ocean ecosystems, discovering solutions to real-world problems facing our seas.

What Participants Will Experience

  • Robotics with Spike Prime: Children will learn to build and program the Spike Prime FLL robot, tackling ocean-themed challenges on a competition table.

  • STEM Exploration: Participants will delve into the layers of the ocean, uncovering its mysteries and discovering innovative solutions to ocean-related issues.

  • Project-Based Learning: Students will use the Engineering Design Process to innovate a solution to an ocean-related problem.

  • Public Speaking and Community Service: Beyond robotics, students will develop essential skills in public speaking and community service by presenting their robot and project to various audiences.
